# 71 - [Ether Units](Ether%20Units.md)
 A literal number can take a suffix of wei, gwei (1e9) or ether (1e18) to specify a sub-denomination of Ether

## Slide Deck
- Suffix -> Literal Number
	- wei, gwei, ether
- wei -> Base Unit
- `1 wei == 1`
- `1 gwei == 1e9`
- `1 ether == 1e18`
- Suffix Multiplication by Power of 10

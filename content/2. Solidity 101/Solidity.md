# 1 - [Solidity](Solidity.md)
Solidity is a high-level language for implementing smart contracts on Ethereum (and the blockchains) targeting the [EVM](../1.%20Ethereum101/EVM.md). 

Solidity was proposed in 2014 by [Gavin Wood](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Gavin_Wood) and was later developed by Ethereumâ€™s Solidity team, led by Christian Reitwiessner, Alex Beregszaszi & others.
